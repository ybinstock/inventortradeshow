Visitors

Welcome Attendee!

We are excited for you to step into Inventor Tradeshow where you can see our inventors invention ideas at various stages, from basic sketches to finished products available for sale. Navigate through the tradeshow using our map and select what area you would like to visit by category. You will enter our virtual world of new ideas! Please register for your badge and enter the number that populates within your email into your badge. You will then have a generated badge to enter. You are welcome to stay as long as you like and come back anytime. Once you sign up, you will receive updates of new exhibitors at Inventor Tradeshow! Thank you for visiting.

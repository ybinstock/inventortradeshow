Exhibitors

Welcome inventor, to Inventor Tradeshow. Have your invention idea seen by buyers, investors, licensees, manufacturers, the general public and anyone that loves inventions!

Our tradeshow is open 24/7 everyday of the year! You can set up your customized booth with your product and information, select up to 3 category rooms to include your booth in. Once approved, you will be issued a booth number and link to your booth to share and promote your product and be seen by everyone! We look forward to having you as an exhibitor at the Inventor Tradeshow!
